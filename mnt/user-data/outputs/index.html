Hi, I am an environmental building designer interested in solving climatic problems using building science research and data-driven analysis. My work focuses on creating thoughtful, climate-reponsive, high-perfomance buildings through comprehensive evaluation of material health, embodied and operational carbon, peak energy loads, and visual comfort.
