Indian National War Memorial

Year: 2017 Type: Competition Collaboators: Kumar Consultants Architects

This shortlisted competition entry reimagines the war memorial as both a space of remembrance and ecological regeneration. Located at India Gate in New Delhi, the design merges symbolic significance with the restoration of landscape systems.

The design centers on hexagonal geometry, a form that carries deep symbolic weight in Indian history and philosophy. Memorial walls radiate from the center, their arrangement drawing from sacred geometric patterns. These symbolic forms become physical structures, creating pathways through ornamental gardens and formal lawns. The memorial walls, rendered in red, guide visitors through sequences of reflection and gathering spaces. The project proposes that memorials need not isolate themselves from their environment, instead, they can actively participate in healing both cultural memory and ecological systems, allowing the landscape to regenerate alongside collective remembrance.
